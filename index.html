Export your Lexi job dates as a calendar file (.ics) and import it into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook — in seconds.

1 Press Download lexi-jobs.ics above
2 Open your calendar app:
Google → Settings → Import

Lexi's Reference System

Every document gets a unique reference number automatically. It follows this format:

LEXI-103-270526

LEXI - your brand on every document sent to a customer.

103 - sequential job number, starting at 100 and going up with every new job.

270526 - the date the job was created (27 May 2026).

You can overwrite this with your own reference if you prefer a different system.
