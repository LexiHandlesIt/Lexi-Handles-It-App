In plain English:
Right now the tradesperson has to decide upfront whether this is an Estimate, Quote or Invoice — before they've even saved the job. In reality, almost every job starts as an Estimate and converts later.

The recommended approach: always save as Estimate when creating. Then, inside the customer dashboard, a simple row of buttons lets you convert it forward — Estimate → Quote → Invoice → Receipt — as the job naturally progresses. No more forcing a decision before it needs to be made.
